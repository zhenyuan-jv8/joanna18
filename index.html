Happy 18th Birthday, Joanna!

It honestly feels surreal to be writing this letter. We've known each other since junior high school, and looking back, it's amazing to see how much we've both grown over the years. Life has taken us in different directions — many of our close friends, including me, moved out of HCM to pursue other opportunities for senior high school, while you stayed. Even though distance changed our daily routines and the moments we used to share, I've always admired how strong, independent, and determined you remained through it all.

As you celebrate this important milestone, I want you to know how proud I am of everything you've accomplished. You have always been one of the smartest people I know, but what stands out even more is your hard work. Your achievements were never simply handed to you — you earned them through countless hours of effort, perseverance, and dedication. You have always been passionate about your goals, and it shows in everything you do.

One thing I admire most about your journey is your incredible comeback during senior high school. You were always capable, but I feel like SHS became your time to truly shine. Seeing you graduate as the 1st Honorable Mention was inspiring and well-deserved. It was proof that consistency, determination, and belief in yourself can lead to amazing results. Watching you achieve that made me genuinely happy because I know how much effort you put into your studies and how hard you worked to get there.

And now, another exciting chapter begins. Congratulations on receiving a full scholarship grant at NU! That is such a huge achievement, and it reflects not only your academic excellence but also your character and commitment to your future. As you pursue Accountancy as an incoming freshman, I wish you nothing but success. There will be challenges ahead, long nights of studying, and moments when things may seem overwhelming, but if there's one thing I know about you, it's that you never give up. I believe you will do great things in college and continue making everyone proud.

I also want to congratulate you on another special milestone — your first relationship. Having a boyfriend is a new experience, and I'm happy that you're able to share this stage of your life with someone important to you. I wish both of you happiness, understanding, growth, and lasting support for one another. May your relationship continue to bring you joy and help you become even better versions of yourselves.

As you turn 18, I hope you continue chasing your dreams fearlessly. May you be blessed with good health, meaningful opportunities, genuine friendships, and countless reasons to smile. I also wish the very best for your family, who have undoubtedly supported and encouraged you throughout your journey. May your home continue to be filled with love, peace, and blessings.

Thank you for all your efforts, your resilience, and for being someone who inspires the people around you. You have come so far, and this is only the beginning. The future holds so many wonderful things for you, and I cannot wait to see everything you accomplish.

Happy 18th Birthday once again. Congratulations on all that you have achieved, and here's to even greater successes, unforgettable memories, and more blessings to come.
